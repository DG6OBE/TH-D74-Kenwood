__Set/Read GPS sentence__

Set the GPS sentence:

	GS p1,p2,p3,p4,p5,p6

Get GPS sentence:

	GS

Returns: p1,p2,p3,p4,p5,p6
	
|p1|sentence
|---|---|
|0|$GPGGA off
|1|$GPGGA on

|p2|sentence
|---|---|
|0|$GPGLL off
|1|$GPGLL on

|p3|sentence
|---|---|
|0|$GPGSA off
|1|$GPGSA on

|p4|sentence
|---|---|
|0|$GPGSV off
|1|$GPGSV on

|p5|sentence
|---|---|
|0|$GPRMC off
|1|$GPRMC on

|p6|sentence
|---|---|
|0|$GPVTG off
|1|$GPVTG on
